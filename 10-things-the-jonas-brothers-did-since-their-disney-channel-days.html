10 Things The Jonas Brothers Did Since Their Disney Channel Days

 Posted on August 17, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  998 words  |  Larita Shotwell

When the Jonas Brothers were formed back in 2005 no one could expect that 15 years into the future they would be one of the most successful pop-rock boy bands out there. Kevin, Joe, and Nick may have started out on the Disney Channel but over the past 15 years, they have certainly grown from teen popstars into serious musicians who won plenty of awards over the years.

Today’s list takes a look at just what the Jonas Brothers have been up to since their days on the Disney Channel — so keep on scrolling to see how their private lives, as well as their careers, have changed!

10 All Three Brothers Got Married — Kevin To Danielle Deleasa, Joe To Sophie Turner, And Nick To Priyanka Chopra

Kicking the list off is the fact that all three brothers have gotten married since their Disney Channel days. The first one to get married was Kevin Jonas who married former hairdresser Danielle Deleasa in December 2009, Nick Jonas was the next one as he married actress Priyanka Chopra in December 2018, and last but not least — Joe Jonas married his wife, Game of Thrones star Sophie Turner, in May 2019! The Jonas Brothers may have dated quite a few famous ladies over the years but it finally seems as if all three of them have settled down.

9 And Kevin And Joe Already Have Children

Not only did the Jonas Bros decide to tie the knot with their significant others — but some of them also have offspring. Kevin Jonas and his wife Danielle welcomed their first daughter in 2014 and their second one in 2016, while Joe Jonas and Sophie Turner welcomed their child in 2020 and a photo of it is yet to be shared. Currently, there are plenty of rumors about Priyanka Chopra being pregnant, so who knows — perhaps Nick Jonas will become a father soon as well!

 

8 The Brothers Embarked On Pretty Successful Solo Careers

Once the Jonas Bros said goodbye to Disney the band decided to go on a hiatus in 2011, and they used the next couple of years to focus on their solo careers.

Related: Everything Taylor Momsen’s Been Up To Since Her Gossip Girl Days

Anyone who followed the Jonas Bros post-breakup knows that while Nick and Joe still had plenty of success within the industry — Kevin certainly seemed to have shifted more of his focus towards his private life and his family.

7 And Joe Jonas Even Joined Another Band — DNCE

While Nick Jonas continued creating music creating music and performing by himself, Joe Jonas decided to join another band — the dance-rock band DNCE. With it, Joe had quite a bit of success, and their debut single "Cake by the Ocean" was without a doubt one of the biggest hits in 2015. And who knows, perhaps DNCE also decides to reunite and some point and give their fans a few more hits!

6 They Reunited In 2019 And Gave Their Fans A Heartwarming Documentary

In 2019 the Jonas Brothers announced their very anticipated comeback and soon afterward the band also released their fifth studio album Happiness Begins. Together with the comeback, the Jonas Brothers also released a documentary titled Chasing Happiness which certainly gave their fans plenty of insight into what has been going on in their lives in the past couple of years.

5 And Together The Brothers Went On Tour

While fans absolutely love every single Jonas Brothers music video that the band ever shot — there is no doubt that seeing the siblings perform on stage together is just a whole different experience. In 2019, the brothers embarked on their 10th concert tour called Happiness Begins which began in August 2019 in Miami Florida and concluded in February 2020 in Paris, France.

4 Nick Performed On Broadway And Gave Acting A Chance

There are certainly quite a few celebs the many may not know actually did Broadway — and Nick Jonas is certainly one of them.

Related: Everything Millie Bobby Brown Has Done Since Stranger Things Premiered

The famous musician actually performed on Broadway back when he was a young child, however, in 2012 — during his solo career — Nick decided to return to Broadway and he played J. Pierrepont Finch in the musical How to Succeed in Business Without Really Trying.

3 Kevin Explored The World Of Reality Television

During the Jonas Bros hiatus, Kevin Jonas decided to explore the world of reality television as he and his wife were the lead cast members of the E! show Married to Jonas which premiered in 2012. As fans who watched it will remember — the show had two seasons and after the second one it was unfortunately canceled.

2 And Joe Joined The Australian Version Of The Voice

Another brother who sort of explored the world of reality television is Joe Jonas. While Joe didn’t necessarily have a show revolving around his life — he did join the popular competition reality television show The Voice Australia as a regular coach during its season seven in 2018. As expected, Joe did a great job and no one would be surprised to see him as a coach or a judge in a singing competition again.

1 And Lastly, They Said Goodbye To Their Disney Channel Image

There is absolutely no doubt that the Jonas Bros had plenty of defining moments in their career and one of them was saying goodbye to their Disney Channel image. Yes, they rose to fame as teen heartthrobs but nowadays they are well-respected musicians and grown men whose lives as well as music have drastically changed. Of course, the majority of their fans grew up with them — and there is no doubt that they will continue to support the Jonas Bros for the rest of their career!
