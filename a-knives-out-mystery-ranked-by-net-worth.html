In 2019, Rian Johnson released Knives Out in theaters for a short period before its release on Netflix and made a whopping $312 million from a $40 million budget. The film was not only nominated for its Original Screenplay at the Academy Awards and British Film Awards. Still, it was also greenlit for two sequels, with Netflix receiving the rights for $469 million. Daniel Craig returned as Detective Benoit Blanc, and A-list actors were cast alongside him in a classic mystery-murder case with suspicion on everybody. The story revolves around Craig’s character Blanc who is invited to investigate the murder of Cassandra Brand. Glass Onion premiered at the Toronto International Film Festival and met with positive reviews.

From Kathryn Hahn and Edward Norton to musical stars Leslie Odom Jr. and Janelle Monáe, Glass Onion: A Knives Out Mystery has one of the best cast line-ups, with each actor bringing their exceptional talent to the screen. Let’s look at the Glass Onion cast and their net worth.

Madelyn Cline ($800,000)

Madelyn Cline plays the supporting role of Whiskey in Glass Onion, who is the girlfriend of Dave Bautista’s Duke. While there is no information about her personality, it remains a mystery for a while. Cline began working in show business through television commercials and has bagged recurring roles in projects such as Stranger Things and The Originals. The actress received her first significant role in 2020 with the action-adventure show Outer Banks.

Kathryn Hahn ($2 million)

While not many artists can dabble between comedy and drama, Kathryn Hahn has mastered the art of being a woman of many talents. She has worked in numerous comedy films as a supporting actress and received critical acclaim for her role as a nosy neighbor in Marvel’s WandaVision, as noted by Deadline. Hahn takes on a political role in Glass Onion as the Governor of Connecticut.

Jessica Henwick ($5 million)

Jessica Henwick became a familiar face after appearing as a recurring supporting cast member in Game Of Thrones as Nymeria Sand. The show's prominence helped her elevate her resume and land roles in Iron Fist, Star Wars: The Force Awakens, and The Matrix: Resurrections. The film sees Henwick play another supporting role as Peg, the assistant of Kate Hudson’s Birdie, as stated by the Hollywood Reporter.

Leslie Odom Jr. ($10 million)

Leslie Odom Jr. has been a prominent name on Broadway since his debut in 1998. In 2016, he earned worldwide acclaim for starring as Aaron Burr in the massively successful Broadway musical Hamilton. The musical won him a Tony Award and a Grammy Award. Odom has slowly ventured into film and television and will appear as scientist Lionel Toussaint in Glass Onion.

Janelle Monáe ($12 million)

A prominent character in the film, Janelle Monáe, appoints Craig’s Benoit to investigate the murder of her sister Cassandra. The songstress is known for contributing to music, acting, and activism. While Monáe has not worked in many films, each project of hers has been successful, including the Oscar-winning film Moonlight and the Oscar-nominated movie Hidden Figures.

Dave Bautista ($16 million)

Dave Bautista became a global star after beginning his career as a wrestler in WWE in the early 2000s. He retired in 2010 and shifted his focus to movies. He made minor appearances in projects and received recognition for his supporting role as Mr. Hinx in the James Bond film Spectre, as mentioned by Variety. Beloved for his role as Drax in the Guardians Of The Galaxy series of the MCU, he reunited with Craig to star in Glass Onion as Duke Cody, a Twitch influencer.

Kate Hudson ($80 million)

While Kate Hudson has taken a step back from acting over the last few years, she returned to movies in the Knives Out sequel as a former model/fashion designer Birdie Jay. Coming from a family with several actors, Hudson began working from a young age and received critical acclaim for her role in Almost Famous in 2000, even winning a Golden Globe. Additionally, she has been co-owner of the fitness brand Fabletics since 2013, worth $500 million.

Daniel Craig ($160 million)

Daniel Craig reprised his role as Detective Benoit Blanc in the sequel, much to everyone’s delight. According to Collider, the actor has signed a $100 million deal with Netflix to star in future releases of the film. This is a second big franchise for him, as he is best known for playing the iconic role of James Bond in five movies between 2006 and 2021. His other notable projects include The Girl With The Dragon Tattoo and Cowboys & Aliens.

Edward Norton ($300 million)

Edward Norton began his career in theater productions before moving to the cinema with his critically-acclaimed debut in Primal Fear. Along with playing the popular Marvel character Hulk, he has received recognition for his work in Fight Club and American History X. In Glass Onion, Norton is cast as billionaire Miles Bron who runs a technology company.

Other cast members include Noah Segan as Derol, Dallas Roberts as Devon Debella, and Jackie Hoffman as Ma Codie. The film also has various cameos throughout the duration, which have remained a secret. Glass Onion: A Knives Out Mystery is set to release on Netflix on December 23, 2022.
