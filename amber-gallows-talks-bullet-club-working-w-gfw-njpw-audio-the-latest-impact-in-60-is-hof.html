Amber Gallows Talks Bullet Club

The “Bullet Babe” Amber (O’Neal) Gallows recently participated in an exclusive interview with Diva Dirt. 

Amber talks about her involvement with the popular Bullet Club stable, working alongside her husband, Bullet Club member and former WWE/TNA star Doc Gallows, making history in New Japan Pro Wrestling and much more. In addition, Amber also talks about her match against Leva Bates at Shine 28, life as a married pro wrestler, Global Force Wrestling, a possible return to Shimmer, and much more.
