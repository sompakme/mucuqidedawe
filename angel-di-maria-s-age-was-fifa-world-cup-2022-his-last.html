Angel Di Maria’s age

Angel Di Maria is a famous footballer who plays for his team, Argentina.

It was confirmed that he was born in Rosario, Argentina on February 14, 1988. As of 2022, this makes him 34 years old. 

He is a right-winger, but sometimes he gets the role of left-winger and an attacker role in the midfield. He is a left-footed player.  

At the age of seven, he enrolled in a local club’s youth academy, and later, at the age of 13, he joined the youth academy of Rosario Central. 

Turning 17, he successfully made it to his first team squad, which marked his professional debut.

Everyone was impressed by his incredible performance in the 2007 FIFA U-20 World Cup, and then he signed for Benfica in the summer after moving to Europe. 

In 2008, he was selected for the squad to play in the Beijing Olympics before making his senior debut in Argentina.

On September 6, 2008, he made his senior debut in the Argentina squad.

At that time, Argentina had a match with Paraguay, which ended in a draw of 1-1.
