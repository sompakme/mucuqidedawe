Bad Bunny, real name Benito Antonio Martínez Ocasio, may appear to have become famous quickly, but those who have been fans from the beginning know better. This Puerto Rican singer and rapper had been a talented star for quite some time before his music dominated the charts. Some even forget he had a $40 million lawsuit against him while dating Kendall Jenner.

In 2018, Puerto Rican rapper Bad Bunny, who got his name from a picture when he was just a child, achieved massive success with his hit song I Like It. The song featured collaborations with Cardi B and J Balvin. The single quickly rose to the top of the Billboard 100 charts and introduced Bad Bunny to a larger audience in the United States. As a result of the song's success and his already-established career in Latin America, Bad Bunny's career skyrocketed. Today, he has amassed an impressive net worth thanks to his many accomplishments in music.

With an impressive catalog consisting of five studio albums, one mixtape, one extended play, one compilation album, 69 singles, and 32 features, Bad Bunny is undoubtedly making a significant impact in the music industry.

It's therefore no wonder that Bad Bunny is now worth a staggering $40 million! So how does that rank with other Latin superstars? Let’s find out!

8 What Is Karol G’s Net Worth?
