Brantley Gilbert Marries Girlfriend Amber Cochran

 Posted on August 31, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  232 words  |  Martina Birk

Off the market! Brantley Gilbert married his girlfriend Amber Cochran at his Georgia home, his rep confirms to Us Weekly.

Cochran and Gilbert have been together for years. In fact, the country crooner revealed during an interview with E! News, who was first to break the news of their marriage, that Cochran was the one that got away.

"I met her 10 years ago. I met her in church, and long story short, we were on and off for five years, mostly on,” said Gilbert, who was previously engaged to Jana Kramer until splitting in August 2013. “Losing her, it was a straight downhill jolt at first…She's the one that got away for five years, and they always say if you love something you've got to let it go, and if it's meant to be, it'll come back. It's so cliché, but now it happened to me. So I'm a believer."

His now-wife, who inspired many of his hits including “More Than Miles” and “You Don’t Know Her Like I Do,” accepted his proposal back in October.
