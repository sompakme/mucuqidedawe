Bunny Eyez on Shark Tank: An Update on the Journey

Bunny Eyez made a captivating appearance on Shark Tank, showcasing its innovative glasses that have taken the eyewear market by storm. The founders, Jenny Hutt, Stacy Fritz, and Andrea, pitched their business seeking a $200,000 investment for 10% equity, presenting their unique eyewear designs that combine fashion and functionality.

The entrepreneurs’ pitch caught the attention of the sharks, who were impressed with the versatility and style of Bunny Eyez glasses. After a series of negotiations and compelling discussions, Daymond John made an enticing offer of $200,000 for 20% equity and a $2 royalty until the investment is recouped – an offer the Bunny Eyez founders gladly accepted.

This partnership with Daymond John has not only infused Bunny Eyez with the required capital but also brought invaluable mentorship and guidance to the brand. Daymond’s expertise and industry connections have propelled Bunny Eyez’s journey, fueling its growth and contributing to its success in the fiercely competitive business world.
