Del Bigtree Biography

Del Bigtree is an American film producer as well as the CEO of the anti-vaccination group Informed Consent Action Network. He was born and brought up in Boulder, Colorado, the United States as Del Matthew Bigtree. He had produced the film Vaxxed: From Cover-Up to Catastrophe, centered on the alleged unsubstantiated connection between vaccines and autism, The Doctor as well as discredited views of Andrew Wakefield. His appeal as a public speaker, with no medical training has made him the most prominent voice in the anti-vaccination movement.
