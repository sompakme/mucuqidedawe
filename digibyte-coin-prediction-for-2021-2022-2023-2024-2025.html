What Is DigiByte?

DigiByte crypto is an open-source blockchain and asset creation platform. The platform is designed to battle against important cryptocurrencies such as Litecoin and XRP by giving importance to cybersecurity and scalability. 

DigiByte has its native token, DGB. The token is utilized to pay fees to those who run the network. And also, DGB intends to offer payments and services to its smart contracts.
