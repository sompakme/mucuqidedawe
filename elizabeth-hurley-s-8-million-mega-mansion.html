Hurley and her then-fiance famed Australian cricketer Shane Warne had bought the luxurious Georgian estate in 2012 before their alleged breakup in 2013. The property is notably known as Donnington Hall and is situated in Ledbury, Herefordshire, 29 miles from the Welsh border. The 17th-century English manor is historically significant and can never be knocked down or replaced. The sprawling grounds boast five houses, including the main dwelling, a coach house, horse stables, a tennis court, and a private fishing lake.

The current value of the English countryside splendor is approximately $11 million. In 2018 there was a burglary incident that prompted upgraded security. The estate is now surrounded by exclusive on-site guards, hi-tech CCTV cameras, and locked private gates for entry.

Donnington Hall Is A Protected Historical Property

The royal estate sits magnificently on 187 acres, and the main house comes with thirteen bedrooms and five bathrooms. The 17th-century Georgian manor is a protected historical site that can never be replaced. According to Daily Mail, apart from the primary residence, there are four cottages, twelve stables, and one coach house.

The perfectly manicured landscape has a private driveway and highly guarded locked ramparts. The Serving Sara actress had expressed a strong desire to delicately refurbish the estate along with her then-fiance, Shane Warne, in 2012. The power couple had even drafted a permission letter to the council. They had requested to repair the grounds and the manor from centuries of damage through historical intervention. Sadly the couple split in 2013, and the plans were not carried out.

Donnington has 21 acres of woodland, multiple lawns, vast green grounds, and a large poolside area. The home's interior is finely finished in the English countryside and has a stately ambiance. One of the main living rooms features a warm fireplace nook, the mantle showcases intricate design, and a leopard print rug compliments the gold accents of the vintage furniture.

According to Closer Weekly, large Georgian windows let in ample daylight and fresh air. The hallways and room walls boast imposing gold-framed mirrors, and the family room sofas radiate in an elegant white with colorful velvet pillows. One of the main staircases comes with a heavy wooden banister, and the beautiful model is often seen posing on the traditional wooden steps. The master bedroom and bathroom, too, have a crisp white ambiance. There is a whole room space for a luxurious walk-in closet.

A Hidden Sanctuary 

According to Mirror, Elizabeth spent the lockdown in the royal fortress with her family. Her 80-year-old mother, an elderly aunt, sister Kate, son Damian, friends, and two dogs had quality time during the pandemic in the royal estate. She spent much of her time during that period doing her favorite hobby - gardening. The grand grounds have a private greenhouse, an orchard with various fruit trees, and a vegetable garden. The Estee Lauder model shared on social media that she was busy harvesting bottles of jam from her fresh home farm produce.

In December 2021, Hurley was recouping from an ankle injury on the posh property. Despite the sore ankle, she was seen posting party pictures throughout the holiday season. The countryside's fresh air and the laid-back atmosphere of Donnington Hall aided her quick recovery.

A Colossal Entertaining Estate

The gourmet kitchen inside the stately property is a chef’s paradise and well-equipped to prepare meals for large gatherings. The home bar features velvet furnishings, leopard prints, and ornate animal showpieces done in gold. The ambiance is all feminine pink with an eye-catching flamingo statue. An extensive wine cellar in the basement proves to be every oenophile’s delight. A private fishing lake on the property comes as an additional picnic and breakfast spot for dreamy summer afternoons. The large pool area has wooden sun loungers, wicker tub chairs, and whimsical outdoor lamps. The exterior gardens are perfectly manicured for yoga sessions and outdoor activities. There is a tennis court for fitness and twelve stables with horses for riding on the royal grounds.

Elizabeth loves to barbecue by the poolside for her guests and family, according to The Sun, the stunning actress enjoys posing for glamor modeling pictures against the gorgeous backdrop of her stately home. She regularly posts videos of garden workouts for her fans on social media. The estate also comes with secluded woodlands with dense trees and a cozy fire pit for social gatherings. The Then Came You actress is known to be a gracious host and enjoys entertaining guests every twice a week in her idyllic English manor. She is known to have entertained the now Duchess of Sussex Meghan Markle, David Beckham, Holly Willoughby, and many other celebrities on her famous lawns.

Elizabeth is living the life of royal luxury at Donnington Hall along with her son Damian. A countryside estate packed with world-class amenities is paradise on earth, and this $11 million property ticks all the boxes on a dream house bucket list.
