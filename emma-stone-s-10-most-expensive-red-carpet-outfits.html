Emma Stone became a famous face for Hollywood movies after her debut in 2007 as the lead in Superbad. Her witty charm and good looks have enamored the world over the years as she starred in comedies and dramas like Easy A, The Amazing Spiderman, La La Land, Crazy, Stupid, Love, and many more. Along with dominating the movie screen, Stone is known to perfect each of her carpet outfits that reflect her quirky yet elegant personality. With the help of her stylist Petra Flannery, the actress has refined her fashion over the years, wearing pieces that suit her style that she can wear with confidence.

Emma Stone loves to champion every red carpet look she has donned, from wearing an outfit worthy of an Oscar winner to making every head turn at Film Festivals and movie premieres. Let’s take a look at some of her most expensive and memorable red carpet outfits.

2016 Critics’ Choice Awards

Emma Stone dazzled at the 2016 Critics’ Choice Awards wearing a custom Roland Mouret Edmonson gown. The actress had paired her outfit with a Christian Louboutin satin clutch, a Cartier trinity ring that costs upwards of $6,300, and a pair of Jimmy Choo heels worth $525. Stone brought glamor to the red carpet, following the trend of wearing cut-out detail dresses like many of her fellow actresses.

2012 Academy Awards

Known for her supporting role in the critically acclaimed movie The Help, Emma Stone presented at the 2012 Oscars ceremony. As stated by US Magazine, the actress had donned a custom Giambattista Valli dress, inspired by Nicole Kidman’s iconic Balenciaga outfit at the 2008 Academy Awards. She had paired her dress with Louis Vuitton jewels.

2016 Venice International Film Festival

Emma Stone opened the Venice International Film Festival in style as she dazzled at the premiere of her critically acclaimed movie La La Land. The actress shone in an Atelier Versace gown embellished with sea-foam ribbon. She accessorized her outfit with metallic Jimmy Choo sandals, Selim Mouzannar-designed rings, and star-shaped earrings.

2014 Venice International Film Festival

Choosing an elegant look for a day event, Emma Stone brought glamor to the opening day at Venice Film Festival for her movie Birdman. According to Cosmopolitan, the actress put her best foot forward, dressed in a custom dark bottle-green Valentino gown that featured a plunging neckline. Keeping accessories to a minimum, the actress styled her cropped hair in subtle waves to complete the look.

Battle Of The Sexes London Premiere

Known to wear head-turning outfits at premieres, Emma Stone wore a whimsical gown at the 61st BFI London Film Festival for her movie Battle Of The Sexes. Inspired by the vintage, she wore a white Louis-Vuitton gown with ruffled sleeves, open shoulder, and silver detailing. The actress was then announced as the newest brand ambassador for Louis Vuitton in 2017.

2017 Golden Globes

Dressed as a winner, Emma Stone wowed the crowd at the 2017 Golden Globes, where she was nominated in the Best Actress category for her work in La La Land. Petra Flannery styled her look, and she dressed the starlet in a nude Valentino gown with a plunging necklace embellished with metallic silver stars. Finishing simple, she paired her outfit with a Tiffany & Co. necklace.

2015 Academy Awards

Counted as one of the best Oscars outfits of all time by Huffington Post, Emma Stone came to win the red carpet in her custom Elie Saab gown. The long-sleeved chartreuse dress had an open back and leg slit that became the most talked-about part of the entire outfit. The custom dress was auctioned off in 2020 to raise money for Beirut at a charity auction.

2017 BAFTA

No other actress had dominated an award season as much as Emma Stone in 2017. Giving one dazzling outfit after another, the actress collected her Best Lead Actress BAFTA dressed in a Chanel Couture. The smoked gray outfit was made from organza and embroidered with 18,000 elements, including mirror shards and crystal stones that required 1,100 hours to create.

2019 Academy Awards

Dressed in her go-to brand Louis Vuitton, Emma Stone looked stunning in a custom-made striking gown intricately designed with exaggerated shoulders and heavy embroidery of beads, sequins, and pearls. As mentioned by Harper’s Bazaar, the outfit was created over 712 hours and additional 12 hours to adjust the measurements after shrinkage. Stone had finished her look with Louis Vuitton jewels.

2017 Academy Awards

The actress that took home one of the biggest honors of the night, Emma Stone, looked every bit an Oscar winner in her shimmering gown. The actress paid tribute to old Hollywood as she dressed in a flapper gown designed in a 1920s style. The custom-beaded gown by Givenchy was designed under Riccardo Tisci, who required a team of 11 and 1,700 hours to make the stunning gown.

From wearing outfits that fit the trend to creating a trend herself, Emma Stone has had a powerful transition in Hollywood. The actress has dominated the red carpet during important award shows that draw public attention. The actress will be next seen dazzling the red carpet for her upcoming movie Babylon set for a December 2022 release.
