Get Inspired By Kylie Jenner's Sneaker Collection, Including Her Ultra Rare Nikes

 Posted on July 12, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1105 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Kylie Jenner has established massive popularity as a fashion symbol in recent years. Not just because of her lion head outfit either. It's mostly due to the fact that she is part of the Kardashian family, is a top model, and has excellent taste, but also because of her dominating social media presence. She is an influencer on another level. She uses social media (mainly Instagram, where she gets paid quite well) to share glimpses of her wardrobe with followers. It might be a red carpet look, matching outfits, or a photo shoot. She is notorious for wearing sought after clothes from high-end fashion labels and for her love of shoes.

Jenner has worked with a lot of sportswear companies over the years to sell her own designs and collections. She has had collaborations with Puma and Adidas. During those times, her contract stated she had to wear those shoes, obviously. Outside contracted hours, though? Kylie Jenner loves her Nikes. Even her beautiful children get cute little Nikes! It's no surprise; after all, Nike leads the way in all lines of fashion. They bring in more money per year than any fashion house or department store.

Kylie Jenner has an exclusive shoe collection that keeps expanding every year, from donning Dior x Nike, which is a dream for many, to her Travis Scott collection, which she of course got before they were available to anyone else. Her shoe obsession is worth over a million dollars, and that's just sneakers. Below, we are taking a closer look.

10 Kylie Jenner's Ultra Rare Nikes

The Dior x Air Jordan 1 High, which fills the niche between street-ready athletic wear and elite fashion, is part of a bigger collaboration that includes footwear, apparel, and accessories. The famous sneaker is reimagined by designer Kim Jones using an Italian leather upper in white and grey with hand-painted edges. A Dior stitched tongue, a Nike Swoosh in Dior Oblique jacquard, and ice outsoles that show a Dior Wings emblem on each shoe are evidence of the dual branding touches. The Dior x Air Jordan 1 High is a limited edition sneaker that was released in April 2020, and it quickly became one of the most sought-after sneakers of the year. With only 8,500 pairs made, the shoe is a rare find for sneakerheads and fashion enthusiasts alike.

9 Kylie Jenner's Ozweego Adidas Sneakers

Adidas Ozweego Sneakers

Because of their sculpted Adiprene cushioning and fluid lines, these Adidas OZWEEGO shoes are eye-catching wherever you go. They are modeled after the adventurous running shoes of the 1990s, so they might look a little familiar. They have, however, been distorted. This is to make them look futuristic, so if they come off as a little unexpected, that's why.

8 Kylie Jenner's Puma Phenom Sneakers

Puma Women's Phenom Sneakers

The Phenom combines cutting-edge style with real performance advantages. The characteristic midfoot strap dominates the top, which is completed with distinctive, feminine details. The high top silhouette draws attention and provides more supported ankles. The collar has a mono mesh insert to increase ventilation.

7 Kylie Jenner's Adidas Grand Court Sneakers

Adidas Grand Court Sneakers

These shoes update classic sport silhouettes from the past by drawing inspiration from them. The shoes' supple leather covering gives them a casual appearance. The sides flash with the iconic 3-Stripes. Every step is comfortable due to the plush midsole cushioning.

These shoes are perfect for those who want to achieve a stylish and comfortable look. They are versatile and can be worn with various outfits, making them a great addition to any wardrobe.

6 Kylie Jenner's Adidas Superstar Shell Tops

Adidas Superstar Legacy Sneakers

The Adidas Superstar is the best example of how street wear fashion, creativity, and sports can all live together in harmony. This shoe is so ingrained in our society that it is just as relevant today as it was when it first appeared over 50 years ago as a basketball shoe. The iconic 3-Stripes have held the Superstar together as it has transcended boundaries since its creation in 1969, extending its influence from the hardwood to every region of the world.

The Adidas Superstar, which is sometimes called the shell-toe, has been a part of a number of collaborations over the years. This 15-foot version of the shoe welcomes people from all over the world at Adidas Village in Portland, Oregon.

5 Kylie Jenner's Nike Air Jordan 1 Sneakers

Women's Nike Air Jordan 1 Sneakers

Classic Jordan style soars to new heights with these Air Jordan 1's. Crisp leather, sparkling embellishments, and the high-top collar and platform make these sneakers the winning addition your outfit needs. The oversized, stitched-on Wings "logo" adds flexibility and is inflated for comfort.

4 Kylie Jenner's Adidas Women's Astir Sneaker

Adidas Women's Astir Y2K Shoes

The Women's Adidas Originals Astir Casual Shoes give a steady dose of retro style and were inspired by running sneakers from the 2000s. The era of excess and luxury for runners is alluded to by distinctive embellishments and exaggerated proportions.

3 Steve Madden Women's Cliff Sneakers

Steve Madden Women's Cliff Sneakers

Steve Madden Cliff enthusiasts earned many praises for their footwear due to its comfort, adaptability, and heel that makes you taller. Despite its narrow fit, many wearers adore its adorable, understated style that is both inexpensive and of great quality.

2 Kylie Jenner's Low-Top Nike Basketball Shoe's

Nike Low-top basketball shoe women

Nike air force 1 shadow honors women who make a difference in their communities and serve as role models for younger generations. This attitude is reflected in the sneaker's design, which has a double swoosh, double height, and double strength. The design of the two swoosh, two mud guards, two rear tabs, and two thin eyelets creates a layered appearance that doubles the brand.

The cut-out sole and foam midsole guarantee comfort, flexibility, and lightness. The slightly enlarged midsole, which has the same iconic shape as the first Air Force 1, gives the sneaker its instant recognition and plays with proportions.

1 Vintage Havana High Tops

Vintage Havana Casual Sneakers

There is nothing like these high-tops from Vintage Havana. The star-shaped studs give you a distinctive appearance that is sure to draw attention. Several closures allow you to fully customize the fit, ensuring all-day comfort. These are a favorite of many celebrities, and are just so pretty. They are crafted with prestige and well worth the price-tag.
