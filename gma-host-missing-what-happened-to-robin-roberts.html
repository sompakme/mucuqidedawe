GMA Host Missing What Happened To Robin Roberts?

 Posted on July 7, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  387 words  |  Martina Birk

The news of the GMA host missing has taken over the internet. The hosts of Good Morning America celebrated Jimmy Kimmel’s 20th year as a late-night presenter on Friday’s broadcast, but the main host Robin Roberts was not present.

The 62-year-old host previously went missing on Dec 16, 2022, too.

The host Lara Spencer addressed the hilarious beef between Jimmy and Matt Damon, and the show’s official Twitter account shared a clip of it. The other hosts, however, did not mention or acknowledge Robin’s absence.

Following the widely circulated hookup, Robin has been subtly criticizing co-hosts Amy and TJ through her morning greetings for weeks.

She hasn’t explicitly spoken about the problem on the show as of now. She said on Thursday that she would take a small break from the show, which might account for her disappearance on Friday.

The 62-year-old Robin Roberts posted her daily inspirational video on her Instagram Thursday morning. In it, she discusses a motivational passage and conducts a prayer. She said “hello” to her supporters, “Happy morning! Greetings, glam fam! “and addressed her staff afterward. 

Robin uttered, “You might find yourself in a constricting circumstance that is out of your control. It may become ingrained in your belief system that scarcity, dysfunction, and struggle are the norm if you frequently witness them.”

“The key is to not let the environment get in you. Don’t let mediocrity become the norm.”

Robin added: “Don’t water down your dreams accepting less when you know God has created you for more.”

Following her inspirational speech, Robin read the day’s prayer before announcing that she was traveling to New Zealand. “I’ve been preparing to go to New Zealand with the Glam Fam for a few weeks, and today is finally the day! Next week, we’ll be updating from there, but it might be difficult to post daily.” She spoke. 

With a cheeky wink to the screen, Robin concluded the message by greeting everyone with a wonderful day.

She also discussed being constrained and the risk of adopting unfavorable situations as the norm. Then Robin underlined the significance of maintaining one’s attitude and inner confidence regardless of the surroundings.

She encouraged, “So, start believing bigger. Start taking steps of faith- stretching, growing, learning. Start living larger and God will amaze you, amaze you with his goodness.”
