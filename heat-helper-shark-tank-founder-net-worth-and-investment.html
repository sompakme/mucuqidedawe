What is Heat Helper?

Heat Helper is an innovative device that revolutionizes the way we utilize the heat generated by our electric laundry drying machines. By redirecting warm air from the dryer into the home, this technology offers numerous benefits, including cost savings on heating expenses and positive environmental impact.

When attached to an electric dryer, the Heat Helper device collects and filters the hot air that is usually vented outside. Instead of wasting this valuable heat, Heat Helper redirects it back into the house. This simple yet highly effective innovation allows homeowners to make the most of their dryer’s warmth, reducing the need for additional heating and lowering energy consumption.

How Heat Helper Works

The Heat Helper device is specifically designed to be compatible with electric dryers. It seamlessly attaches to the machine and efficiently captures the hot air it generates during the drying process. By using a filtering system, Heat Helper ensures that only clean, warm air is redirected into the home, free from any lint or contaminants.

Once the hot air is collected, Heat Helper channels it through a specially designed duct system that directs the warmth into different areas of the house. This innovative technology allows homeowners to efficiently heat specific rooms or provide overall warmth throughout their living spaces, depending on their needs.

The Benefits of Heat Helper

The Heat Helper device offers several notable benefits:

  • Energy Savings: By harnessing the heat produced by the dryer and recycling it within the home, Heat Helper reduces the need for additional heating, resulting in significant energy savings.
  • Cost Reduction: With decreased energy consumption, homeowners can expect to see a noticeable reduction in their heating bills, saving money in the long run.
  • Environmental Impact: By utilizing the heat that would otherwise be wasted, Heat Helper contributes to a more sustainable lifestyle by reducing energy waste and carbon emissions.
  • Convenience: With Heat Helper, homeowners can enjoy the warmth generated by their dryer without the need for additional heating devices or systems.

Whether it’s redirecting warmth to a specific room or maintaining overall comfort throughout the house, Heat Helper’s innovative technology provides a practical and efficient solution for maximizing the benefits of your electric dryer.

Heat Helper on Shark Tank

On Season 3 Episode 7 of Shark Tank, Lyle Schuette presented his innovative product, Heat Helper, to the panel of investors. Looking for a $100,000 investment in exchange for a 50% stake in his company, Lyle aimed to showcase the value of Heat Helper and secure a deal.

During his pitch, Lyle revealed that he had already generated sales for Heat Helper. With approximately 680 units sold through his website and major retailers, the product had gained traction in the market.

As part of his valuation, Lyle set Heat Helper’s worth at $200,000, based on his asking price for the investment. The Sharks displayed interest in the product, recognizing its potential benefits and market appeal.

Unfortunately, despite garnering attention from the panel, Lyle did not receive any offers for Heat Helper. The Sharks passed on the opportunity to invest, leading to a missed deal on the show. However, Heat Helper’s appearance on Shark Tank increased its visibility and attracted significant interest from potential customers and online visitors.
