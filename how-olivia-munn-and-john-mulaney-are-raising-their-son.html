In this article, we'll shed light on who Olivia Munn and John Mulaney are, how they got together, and how they are raising their son, Malcolm.

8 Who Is Olivia Munn?

Olivia Munn has been in the entertainment industry since 2004

Olivia Munn is a popular comedy and action actress. Fans might remember her from her brief stint on the hit television show New Girl. Munn starred in the 2020 film Love Wedding Repeat, and she has also appeared in movies such as The Predator, Magic Mike, and X-Men: Apocalypse. Munn dated many celebrities before her relationship and pregnancy with John Mulaney.

Munn actually got her start in the entertainment industry as a television host. She began in 2006 working for a gaming network, and then appeared as a correspondent on The Daily Show with Jon Stewart for many years.

7 Who Is John Mulaney?

John Mulaney is a writer and comic

John Mulaney is a comedian and writer. He began his career as a writer for the highly successful comedy skit show Saturday Night Live. After leaving the show, Mulaney went on to became a very famous stand-up comedian. He has several comedy specials on Netflix, and he also is a part of the streaming network’s original show Big Mouth.

Mulaney was previously married to Anna Marie Tendler. The two tied the knot in 2014, and Mulaney gave hints of their private life in his stand-up specials.

6 John Mulaney Struggled With Addiction

John Mulaney got sober in 2005 but relapsed in 2020

Through his comedy specials, Mulaney has been very open with the public about his substance abuse. He first became sober in September 2005, a choice driven by his lack of control when it comes to alcohol and drugs. Unfortunately, after a decade and a half of sobriety, Mulaney relapsed during the global pandemic.

In December 2020, Mulaney made the decision to check himself into a rehabilitation facility. The decision came after his friends and family held an intervention for his substance abuse. Mulaney is not the only celebrity to struggle with substance abuse. Matthew Perry spent roughly $9 million trying to get sober.

5 When Did John Mulaney And Olivia Munn Start Dating?

John Mulaney and Olivia Munn started dating in July 2021

The timeline of John Mulaney’s separation from ex-wife Anna Marie Tendler and his new relationship with Olivia Munn had fans scratching their heads. Mulaney filed for divorce from Tendler in July 2021, but they had announced their split in May of that year.

Days after the announcement of his divorce, a source revealed to People that Mulaney was seeing Olivia Munn. “This is very new, they’re taking it slowly,” the source revealed. Fans wonder what really went on between Mulaney and Tendler behind closed doors, and if it involved Munn.

4 Do Olivia Munn And John Mulaney Live Together?

Olivia Munn and John Mulaney have lived together since 2021

Though Olivia Munn and John Mulaney have only been officially together since 2021, they had to move fairly quickly in their relationship. The celebrity couple welcomed their son Malcolm Mulaney in November 2021, very quickly after Mulaney confirmed their relationship and Munn’s pregnancy.

Becoming a mother has been a big adjustment for Munn. She had never been particularly interested in having children. Mulaney had also claimed to not want children. In order to tackle the adjustment together, Munn and Mulaney moved in together and have been working on their relationship and rasing their son ever since.

3 Parents Olivia Munn And John Mulaney Love To Gush About Son

Malcolm is a prominent figure on both Olivia Munn and John Mulaney's social media

Neither Olivia Munn nor John Mulaney had been parents before their son Malcolm. Since they are both parents for the first time, it is no surprise they both love to share moments of their son with the world.

Both Munn and Mulaney have shared some adorable photos and videos of Malcolm on their social media. Fans love getting glimpses of their happy family, and Malcolm is certainly a very cute baby.

2 Olivia Munn Lost Sleep Over Son’s First Sickness

Olivia Munn did not sleep for nearly a week when Malcolm was sick

It is a matter of life that kids get sick, and that was no difference for Olivia Munn and John Mulaney’s son Malcolm. Their young child unfortunately felt under the weather in the Summer of 2022, and that meant hell for Munn.

The Newsroom actress Munn did not sleep for days—nearly a whole week—due to her son’s first sickness. Sleep deprivation is a staple in the early days of motherhood and fatherhood.

1 Olivia Munn And John Mulaney's Relationship After Son's Birth

Olivia Munn and John Mulaney are still going strong, having stepped out on the red carpet for the first time together in 2024

Olivia Munn and John Mulaney were very early in their relationship when they learned they were going to be parents. For any new couple, that could be a recipe for disaster. Thankfully for fans of the couple, that was not the case for Mulaney and Munn.

“Olivia and John have become much closer since the birth of their son,” a source revealed to E! News. “They are very committed to each other and raising their son together.
