Inside Michael Evans Behling's Mysterious Romantic And Personal Life

 Posted on August 3, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  811 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Within the three or four years after 2018, Michael Evans Behling became a very familiar face in Hollywood. This was mostly thanks to his involvement as part of the cast of All American on The CW. His character on the show is known as Jordan Baker, a role that he also reprised in a guest capacity in the spin-off series, All American: Homecoming.

Behling’s salary on the show is not yet a matter of public knowledge, but The CW is known to be generous with its star actors. Stephen Amell reportedly takes home $125,000 per episode of Arrow, while stars like Jensen Ackles, Kristen Bell, and Grant Gustin were all paid similar – or even higher – salaries.

All American made its debut on the network in October 2018, with a first season consisting of 16 episodes. Season 5 of the hit coming-of-age sports drama kicked off in October 2022. The second season of All American: Homecoming arrived on The CW in the same month, with the show having first premiered in February 2022.

Speaking about his role in All American, Behling draws parallels between his character and his own personal life, starting with both their upbringing.

8 Does Michael Evans Behling Have Any Siblings?

Like his character Jordan Baker, Michael Evans Behling is biracial. In an interview he gave to Hollywood Life, he explained that this was the fact that made him relate most to Jordan.

Behling grew up in an adoptive home. He has two brothers called Matt and Adam, as well as a sister known as Andrea.

7 Michael Evans Behling Started Out In Sports And Modeling

Michael Evans Behling comes across as a natural on All American, but acting was not always his first port of call. Growing up, he was more interested in sports – another thing he has in common with Jordan Baker.

Behling played baseball, soccer, and volleyball. He also ventured into modeling and even started his own clothing line, known as DesignedAt5AM.

6 What Inspired Michael Evans Behling To Be An Actor?

Having fixated on other interests before, it took a special performance from a special actor to spark his passion in the craft.

Asked by Euphoria Magazine about who his greatest influence was, Behling said: “Heath Ledger. It was his performance in The Dark Knight that had me glued to the big screen [and] shined a light on how powerful acting can be.”

5 Is Michael Evans Behling In A Relationship?

Michael Evans Behling has proven in his budding Hollywood years that he is not a huge fan of PDAs. His lack of romantic dalliances in public has led to widespread speculation that he is single.

In a 2021 interview with Hollywood Mask, however, he hinted that he had a special someone in his life, and also confirmed that he had previously been in a serious relationship.

4 Was Michael Evans Behling Previously Married?

In July 2022, reports emerged that Michael Evans Behling had filed divorce on a secret, five-month marriage. The actor is said to have been in the covert relationship with a woman known as Madison Stanton.

Behling has never commented to confirm or deny the reports of the said marriage.

3 Who Is Michael Evans Behling’s Ideal Woman?

Although the identities of Michael Evans Behling’s past and present romantic partners remain unknown, the actor has been more open with the qualities that would define an ideal woman for him.

In his conversation with Hollywood Mask, he said: “I love when a girl is nice for no reason… to everyone. No underlying reason, she’s just pure and genuine. I eat that up!” Behling also revealed that he was “a sucker” for nice teeth in a woman.

2 How Active Is Michael Evans Behling On Social Media?

Michael Evans Behling is a prolific social media user, with accounts on Instagram, Twitter and TikTok. Being the private man he is, however, most of his interactions are almost exclusively about his work.

Behling has 1.4 million followers on Instagram, nearly 61,000 on Twitter and 2.5 million on TikTok.

1 What Is Michael Evans Behling’s Net Worth In 2022?

Michael Evans Behling’s exact net worth is not known. This is not too surprising, given his still relatively up-and-coming status in the industry. Various outlets do approximate the total value of his wealth to lie somewhere between $500,000 and $1 million.

Besides All American, Behling’s other acting roles have been guest appearances in Empire and Grey’s Anatomy. He also starred in the 2021 teen comedy film, A Cinderella Story: Starstruck.
