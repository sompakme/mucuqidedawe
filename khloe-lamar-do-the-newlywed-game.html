Khloe &amp; Lamar Do The Newlywed Game

 Posted on September 18, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  208 words  |  Martina Birk

Looks like Khloe Kardashian and Lamar Odum are still experiencing the newlywed bliss period. During the cover shoot for the Spring issue of Fearless Magazine, the married pair decided to play the magazine’s version of the Newlywed Game. Check out their answers to the questions below: 

1. When you leave the house, what is your spouse most likely doing?
Khloe said for Lamar- c. napping
Lamar said for Khloe- d. on the phone

a. partying

b. cleaning

c. napping

d. on the phone

e. none of the above

2. What is your spouse’s favorite “comfort food”?
Khloe said for Lamar- candy or chips
Lamar said for Khloe- honey wheat pretzels

3. What would your spouse say his/ her greatest strength would be?
Khloe said for Lamar- his patience, he thinks about his responses versus reacts
Lamar said for Khloe- her passion

4. What’s the one item of clothing your spouse wears that you just can’t stand?
Khloe said for Lamar- Kangol bucket hats
Lamar said for Khloe- nothing

5. What is your spouse’s most irritating habit?
Khloe said for Lamar- Slob Kabob
Lamar for Khloe- Talks a lot
