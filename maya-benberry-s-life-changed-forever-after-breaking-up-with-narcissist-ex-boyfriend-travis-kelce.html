Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift undoubtedly hold special places in the hearts of both Hollywood and the sports world today after he admitted to giving his number to Swift. On the contrary, Maya Benberry, the former flame of the NFL star, has made some startling claims about Kelce, especially since her life took a dramatic turn after parting ways with him.

Benberry is a social media personality, which is why her relationships often play out in the spotlight. After her claims were debunked and she was accused of trying to ride the fame of Swift and Kelce to stay relevant, her story of breaking up with her ex-boyfriend Travis Kelce, whom she had dubbed a narcissist and cheater, became a turning point in her life.

8 What Does Maya Benberry Do For A Living?

Maya Benberry hails from Kentucky and she wears multiple professional hats, as showcased on her Instagram profile. Her diverse roles include being a life coach and an expert in marketing and brand consulting.

She also established herself as a motivational speaker under her brand, Moments with Maya Benberry. According to Screen Rant, Benberry served as a booking manager for musician Derrick Miller. She holds the position of creator and owner at MXM Marketing and oversees its marketing and brand consulting operations.

7 Inside Maya Benberry's Beauty Products

Maya Benberry's foray into the realm of beauty has given rise to her innovative creation, the Benberry Beauty Bar. Within the confines of this beauty line, she shares not only her products but also her extensive knowledge, providing an invaluable resource for individuals seeking guidance in self-care and aesthetics.

Through her Benberry Beauty Bar, she has established herself as a trusted source for those looking to enhance their understanding of beauty and wellness, making her mark in the ever-evolving world of self-care.

6 Maya Benberry Accused Travis Kelce Of Cheating With Kayla Nicole

Maya Benberry gained fame as a reality TV personality after she appeared as a contestant and winner on Travis Kelce's 2016 E! dating show, Catching Kelce.

Following her victory on Catching Kelce, she and Kelce dated for a few months before ending their relationship. Despite not addressing their split publicly, Benberry later accused the Kansas City Chiefs player, of cheating on her with Kayla Nicole. Kelce and Nicole had an on-and-off relationship spanning from 2017 to 2022.

4 Maya Benberry Starred In Another Reality Show, MTV's Ex On The Beach

2 Maya Benberry Faced Backlash From Taylor Swift Fans After She Allegedly Mocked Her

"But once I caught wind that it was shade, I deleted the comment entirely. I'm a grown-ass woman and I stand on what I say. I am not a coward and if I truly felt that way about her, I would stand on it, just like I am standing on my infidelity answer," she further explained.
