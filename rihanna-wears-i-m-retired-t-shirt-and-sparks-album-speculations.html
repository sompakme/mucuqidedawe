Rihanna Wears &quot;I'm Retired&quot; T-Shirt And Sparks Album Speculations

 Posted on September 17, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  378 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Rihanna may have just sparked even more speculations about a possible (or not possible) album with just one T-shirt.

While out-and-about with A$AP Rocky, the Fenty creator was caught on cam by a paparazzo wearing an “I’m Retired. This is as dressed up as I get” tee. Although it may have just been a vintage fashion choice, that didn’t stop social media users from accusing Rih of being calculated and sending a message about what would be her ninth studio album.

According to Vogue, the shirt is from Peacock Papers, a brand known for their eye-catching slogans emblazoned on apparel.

The billionaire babe spoke on her love for vintage finds with Complex back in April and shared that she’s been hunting for more fashion gems to add to her, Rocky, and their children’s wardrobe.

The mother of two admitted that she has an “obsession” with finding older pieces like Avirex jackets, Ecko jeans, and Power Rangers T-shirts, although it’s more difficult to find those for children.

“Vintage is a hard find for kids,” she said. “I’m obsessed. It’s shocking to see all the good stuff they made back in the day.”

“I just fell to my knees in Bojangles,” one person commented on the paparazzi photo of her. Another speculated, “???
