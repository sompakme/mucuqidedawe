Rumors Surface That John Mulaney Is Paranoid About People Filming His Show

 Posted on September 28, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  489 words  |  Aldo Pusey

John Mulaney has started performing his first live comedy show ever since he checked into a Pennsylvania rehab facility at the end of last year.

According to fan reports on celebrity gossip page @deuxmoi.discussions, the comedian is particularly concerned about people recording the show.

John Mulaney Wants To Keep His New Show Secret

An anonymous source claimed that an unauthorized recording of Mulaney’s show started circulating among his fans, presumably after someone took a video during one of the comedian’s latest shows.

“Someone tried to share a bootleg recording of this highly praised show done by this deux-moi fave,” the source alleged.

They also said the recording was shared with “several interested fans”.

However, Mulaney’s management must have taken action to take it down.

"The platform or his team, unfortunately, took it down fast before it could go viral. This would’ve been a disaster for them,” the source continues.

They then explained the incident has allegedly made Mulaney “paranoid” about people trying to record his show.

“Mulaney’s paranoid about phones at his show. Really f***ing paranoid. Guests have to lock them in a special container,” the source continued.

Fans took Mulaney’s side, explaining that it’s standard for a comedian to keep their show under wraps in the workshop phase.

“It's just not Mulaney, most well known comics don't allow phones at shows these days but especially when they are working on material for their next tour. Industry standard,” one fan wrote.

“It’s make perfect sense because people these days can take things out of context, especially when a comedian is sorting through new material,” another Instagram user commented.

“Seems understandable to me, he's still workshopping and probably feels vulnerable sharing something so personal,” was another comment.

Are John Mulaney And Olivia Munn Expecting A Baby?

Mulaney is back on stage for the first time after checking into a rehab facility over his addiction to alcohol and cocaine. The actor and comedian also announced he is divorcing his wife of six years, artist Annamarie Tendler.

He is now rumored to be in a relationship with actress Olivia Munn.

According to US Weekly, which published a timeline of their relationship, Munn and Mulaney met at church. She also wished him well on his sobriety when his decision to commit to a 60-day program was announced. Back in 2015, Munn apparently hung out with Mulaney and Tendler at a wedding.

A new video suggests that the budding couple is about to welcome a baby together.

“The word on the street is Olivia Munn is pregnant,” New York City comic duo @celebritymemoirbookclub announced on their TikTok page.

The two comedians claim an anonymous source close to Mulaney told them he and Munn are expecting a baby. The rumored couple is reportedly keeping their romance quiet and hasn’t confirmed the news of a baby.
