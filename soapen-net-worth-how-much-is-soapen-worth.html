SoaPen Product Overview

Introducing SoaPen, a kid-friendly hand soap designed to make handwashing fun and engaging. Shaped like a pen, it comes in three vibrant colors: Tangy Tangerine (red), Berry Blast (blue), and Fresh Pear (green). Kids will love the playful design, making hand hygiene an enjoyable experience.

Each SoaPen has a soap capacity of 33 ml, ensuring lasting use before a refill is needed. It’s the perfect size for little hands, promoting independence and building good hygiene habits. Plus, parents can feel confident knowing that SoaPen is free from harmful ingredients such as SLS, parabens, Phthalates, and EDTA.

Using SoaPen is simple and easy. Kids can draw fun patterns or even write on their hands using the soap pen. Then, with a splash of water, they can watch as the soap magically transforms into a gentle lather that rinses away dirt and germs. It’s a creative and effective way to encourage thorough handwashing.

SoaPen is available for both one-time purchase and subscription options, providing flexibility for every family’s needs. Whether you’re looking for a convenient refillable option or prefer the convenience of regular deliveries, SoaPen has you covered.
