The Cast Of Bling Empire: New York, Ranked By Net Worth

 Posted on September 14, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  830 words  |  Larita Shotwell

If you're a big fan of Bling Empire on Netflix then you will be very happy to hear a spin-off series set in New York will soon be streaming. It will star Bling Empire favorite Dorothy Wang, alongside a whole host of new wealthy Asians.

The synopsis, released by Netflix, says: "Meet a fresh group of wealthy, sophisticated and hilarious Asian-Americans from New York City, where the quality of real estate is measured by address, not acreage. Conversations are direct, not coded. And competition – for love, for money, and for power – is fierce. Billionaires, CEOs, and fashion icons - it’s a city where you can be anything you want to be."

So who are these new, wealthy people who we can't wait to meet, fawn over and perhaps even loathe?

8 Extravagant Businessman Stephen Hung's Net Worth Is $400 Million

Stephen Hung is a millionaire businessman who is reportedly the joint chairman of an investment holdings company called The 13 Holdings Limited.

Chinese-born Hung is currently sitting on an impressive $400 million fortune, making him the richest cast member of this reality TV spin-off. Hung's wealth stems from his career as a former banker, property speculator, and connections in the world of business.

He hit the headlines and made history for making the single most expensive Rolls Royce purchase in history!

7 Model And Agent Deborah Hung's Net Worth Is $400 Million

Hung's wife, Deborah Valdez-Hung will also appear on the show. She boasts a long and successful career as a supermodel, who went on to own one of the most exclusive modeling agencies to service Asia and much of Europe, Dreamlands.

Hung's own personal wealth may be significantly less than her husbands but should anything happen in that marriage, we are sure she will become a much richer woman! Her net worth is currently estimated to be $400 million.

6 Real Estate Broker Dorothy Wang Is Worth $10 Million

Dorothy Wang is the daughter of self-made billionaire businessman Roger Wang. She previously appeared on the second season of Bling Empire, Famously Single, and Rich Kids Of Beverly Hills.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Dorothy is worth about $10 million. She is a licensed real estate broker and works for The Agency. She also has a jewelry line named Fabuluxe and a champagne brand called Rich and Bubbly.

5 Model And It Girl Tina Leung's Net Worth Is $1.5 Million

In the trailer for the reality show, Tina is described as a fashion "It girl. Chinese Leung, is a high profile blogger and fashion stylist

Her work has been featured in editorials for Hong Kong’s Harper’s Bazaar, Marie Claire,Elle, and more. She runs her personal blog, Tina Loves and her net worth is approximately $1.5 Million.

4 Lynn Ban, Jewelry Maker To The Stars', Net Worth Is Unspecified

Lynn Ban is known for all things jewelry, making pieces for the likes of Beyoncé and Rihanna. The Singapore born designer launched Genki Sushi franchise in New York, Singapore and Hong Kong in the 1990s.

In 2002, she launched Lynn Ban Vintage at Barneys New York with a curated selection of vintage couture ranging from Dior to Chanel. She followed this with the first pop-up of Lynn Ban Vintage at Harvey Nichols in London and Celux in Tokyo. The latter is a private members-only club in the penthouse of the Louis Vuitton flagship in Omotesando.

Her net worth is unspecified, but we imagine she has done well for herself, working with London-based Michael Halpern on jewellery to complement his AW 2018 collection and dressing some of the most high profiles stars.

3 Fashion Editor Blake Abbie's Net Worth Is Around $1 Million

A fashion editor turned reality star, Blake Abbie started as a photography assistant and later applied to be an editorial assistant. He's now the editor-at-large of A Magazine Curated By, per The Cut.

In 2018, Canadian-born Abbie made his television debut in the Chinese series Meteor Garden playing Thomas. It is thought his net worth is around the $1 million range.

2 Chief Growth Officer Richard Chang's Net Worth Is Unknown

Richard Chang has worked as the Chief Growth Officer for health company Hudson Medical and Wellness since December 2021. Before he worked at The Sleep Spa as a senior vice president, the director of merchandise planning at Calvin Klein and the director at Hastens Beds.

"I have dated half of the population in New York City," he proudly says in the trailer. His net worth is currently unknown.

1 The Bling Empire: New York Premiere Date

Netflix hasn’t released too much information about the upcoming series beyond its first look teaser. We do know that some of Bling Empire: New York Season 1 has already been filmed, so we are expecting the spin-off to be released early in 2023.
