The Cast Of 'The Real Housewives Of Miami,' Ranked By Net Worth

 Posted on September 10, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  860 words  |  Larita Shotwell

After its original 2013 cancellation by Bravo, The Real Housewives of Miami (RHOM) has been revived to TV screens, though has found a new home on the Peacock streaming service, premiering its fourth season in December 2021, to the satisfaction of many Real Housewives fans. Like its predecessor shows in Atlanta and Beverly Hills, or its later additions in Potomac and Salt Lake City, RHOM serves the same purpose of all other Housewives shows, including fun or disastrous events, arguments, and lavish vacations.

As the show has rebooted, its mixture of returning and new cast members are either back or welcomed into the reality TV spotlight. Many wonder what the returning cast members (Alexia Echevarria, Lisa Hochstein and Larsa Pippen) have been up to throughout the years, whereas they are also interested in the backgrounds of its newest additions (Guerdy Abraira, Julia Lemigova and Dr. Nicole Martin) prior to joining the show. Below is a list of the Miami Housewives ranked by their net worth, from lowest to highest.

6 Julia Lemigova - $1 Million

Making headlines for becoming the first housewife in a same-sex marriage (having been married to tennis legend Martina Navratilova since 2014), new Miami housewife Julia Lemigova has an $1 million net worth, according to Meaww. The former model and Miss USSR pageant winner engaged in entrepreneurship in the years after her pageant days came to an end. While any salary or entrepreneur income is unknown, it is estimated that most Housewives make an average of $60,000 their first season, according to Us Weekly.

5 Guerdy Abraira - $2-3 Million

Another newcomer of the show's fourth season, Haiti native Guerdy Abraira makes a living as an event stylist, operating her own business named Guerdy Design, responsible for designing parties, weddings and other events across the Miami area. According to TheCinemaholic, Abraira is estimated to be worth between $2 to 3 million as of December 2021. Her event designs have been seen in major publications such as Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, The New York Times and People magazine.

4 Dr. Nicole Martin - $3 Million

One of the few housewives in the medical field (such as former Dallas housewife Dr. Tiffany Moon and current Orange County housewife Dr. Jen Armstrong), new Miami housewife Dr. Nicole Martin makes a living as an anesthesiologist working within the University of Miami Health System. Shortly after the trailer for the show's return, CelebSaga reported her net worth at $3 million in November 2021, which is shared with her fiancé Anthony Lopez, a Miami insurance attorney and pilot.

Furthermore, her salary is estimated to be around $389,000, as Florida anesthesiologists make anywhere between $339,000 to $443,600, as salaries range considering certifications and years of experience.

3 Larsa Pippen - $10 Million

One of the original housewives from Miami's inaugural season, Larsa Pippen left after her first round on the show, though returned for its revival. As of February 2022, the ex-wife of former all-star basketball player Scottie Pippen has a reported net worth of $10 million.

As part of her income, she has her own jewelry line named Larsa Marie and has an OnlyFans account to interact with fans, where she claims to make $10,000 per day. The mentioning of her OnlyFans page has become a frequent topic on the show's current season. Furthermore, she receives alimony and child support for their younger two children, ages 14 and 17.

2 Lisa Hochstein - $30 Million

Married to Leonard "Lenny" Hochstein one of the top plastic surgeons in Miami, season two onward housewife Lisa Hochstein shares his $30 million net worth. The couple, who happily welcomed two children via surrogate after fertility struggles and the show's initial cancellation, own the Hochstein Medspa, which offers various plastic surgery services. According to the med spa's website, Hochstein has performed over 27,000 breast surgeries, thus giving him the nickname of "The Boob God." Furthermore, the two also own a 20,000-square-foot home on Star Island, one of Miami's most affluent neighborhoods.

"I definitely feel like a Barbie living in a Barbie dream house," said Hochstein in the fourth season premiere. "The thing I love most about my house is, of course, my closet. It is every woman's dream. It's my woman cave."

1 Alexia Echevarria - $30 Million

Considered a fan favorite housewife, Alexia Echevarria's net worth is tied with Hochstein's, as she also has an estimated $30 million net worth, according to Meaww. She previously served as the executive editor of Miami's Venue Magazine, though publication ceased in 2017 after the death of her ex-husband Herman Echevarria.

Nowadays, she operates Alexia + Frankie's Beauty Bar in Surfside, Florida, a salon offering services for hair, makeup, nails, waxing, and more. The salon was named after her and her youngest son, who almost died from a 2011 car accident, though eventually recovered.
