The Italian Job - 2003 ($176.2 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Revving with high-octane thrills, this slick heist flick raced its way to box office success. Mark Wahlberg takes the driver's seat in The Italian Job as Charlie Croker, leading a team of cunning criminals on a mission to steal a fortune. With precision and charisma, Wahlberg's performance had audiences gripping their seats, propelling this incredible movie to a jaw-dropping $176.2 million at the worldwide box office. It's safe to say that this film didn't just steal gold, but also the hearts of moviegoers.

Ted 2 - 2015 ($217.2 Million)

Via Flickr/Cute Wallpapers

Who knew that a foul-mouthed, pot-smoking teddy bear could generate big laughs and even bigger box office numbers? In this uproarious sequel of Ted, Mark Wahlberg reprises his role as John Bennett, the thunder buddy to everyone's favorite stuffed buddy. The hilarious duo embarks on a quest to prove Ted's legal rights as a person, and their escapades had audiences roaring with laughter. Wahlberg's impeccable comedic timing and chemistry with a CGI bear catapulted this movie to a whopping $217.2 million worldwide.

The Departed - 2006 ($289.6 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

In Martin Scorsese's crime masterpiece, The Departed, Mark Wahlberg commands the screen as Dignam, a sharp-tongued and no-nonsense detective. This gritty and complex tale of undercover cops and mob infiltration garnered critical acclaim and box office success, grossing a staggering $289.6 million worldwide. Wahlberg's intense performance alongside a stellar cast earned him an Academy Award nomination and showcased his ability to tackle dramatic roles with a ferocity that leaves a lasting impact.

The Perfect Storm - 2000 ($328.7 Million)

Via Flickr/Foxtel

Prepare for a tempest of heart-pounding adventure in The Perfect Storm as Mark Wahlberg portrays Bobby Shatford, a fearless fisherman facing the wrath of a monstrous storm. Wahlberg's portrayal captures the resilience and determination of a man battling nature's fury, and audiences were captivated by the thrilling tale. With its stunning visual effects and Wahlberg's gripping performance, it's no surprise that this film made waves, sailing to a remarkable $328.7 million at the worldwide box office.

Planet of the Apes - 2001 ($362.2 Million)

Via Flickr/Flixable

In this epic science fiction saga, Planet of the Apes, Mark Wahlberg ventures into a world ruled by intelligent apes. He brings to life the courageous astronaut Leo Davidson, who finds himself in a battle for survival and the fate of humanity. Wahlberg's performance strikes a perfect balance between action and emotion, navigating the treacherous landscapes of this visually stunning film. This interstellar adventure catapulted Wahlberg's career to new heights, and audiences contributed to its impressive $362.2 million worldwide gross.

Uncharted - 2022 ($400.6 Million)

Via Flickr/Empire Weekly

In this action-packed video game adaptation, Uncharted, Mark Wahlberg steps into the shoes of Victor Sullivan, a seasoned adventurer, and mentor to the film's protagonist. Wahlberg's portrayal injects the character with a mix of rugged charm and dry wit, adding depth to the thrilling narrative. With Wahlberg's star power and the film's high-stakes escapades, audiences flocked to theaters, driving this exhilarating movie to a whopping $400.6 million at the worldwide box office.

Ted - 2012 ($556 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Mark Wahlberg teams up with a foul-mouthed teddy bear in Ted, brought to life by Seth MacFarlane's voice acting. The unlikely duo embarks on a journey of friendship, mischief, and uproarious adventures that had audiences in stitches. This raunchy and irreverent comedy resonated with viewers, resulting in a staggering $556 million at the worldwide box office. It's proof that sometimes the most unexpected pairings can lead to blockbuster success.

Transformers: The Last Knight - 2017 ($602.8 Million)

Via Wikimedia Commons

Joining the explosive Transformers franchise, Mark Wahlberg takes on the role of Cade Yeager, a brave inventor caught in the midst of an intergalactic war between Autobots and Decepticons. With Wahlberg's magnetic screen presence and his ability to seamlessly blend action and humor, this thrilling installment captivated audiences worldwide. The film's stunning visual effects and high-octane action sequences propelled it to an impressive $602.8 million at the worldwide box office. Wahlberg's portrayal of Cade added a new dimension to the Transformers saga, leaving fans eagerly awaiting the next chapter.

Transformers: Age of Extinction - 2014 ($1.1 Billion)

Via Flickr/Fun Learning Life

Mark Wahlberg returns to the Transformers universe in this explosive blockbuster that shattered box office records. As Cade Yeager, a rugged inventor, and mechanic, Wahlberg brings his signature charm and intensity to the role. Audiences were enthralled by the film's larger-than-life action sequences and visually spectacular robot battles. With a staggering $1.1 billion in worldwide box office earnings, this installment solidified Wahlberg's status as a blockbuster powerhouse and showcased the enduring popularity of the Transformers franchise.
