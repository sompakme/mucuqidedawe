Tag Team Returning on WWE Smackdown Live Tonight

The Headbangers noted on Twitter they will be returning to WWE Smackdown Live tonight. This will be the third appearance on Smackdown by the tag team this year.

The Rock Named People Magazine’s Sexiest Person Alive

Dwayne “The Rock” Johnson has been named People Magazine’s “Sexiest Person Alive” for 2016. The Rock posted the cover of the issue on Instagram, which you can check out below, and offered the following comments on the honor:

“I said, ‘That’s awesome.’ And then what went through my mind was just how cool and exciting it is. And then I thought, ‘Wow, we’ve pretty much reached the pinnacle.’ I’m not quite too sure where we go from here. I’ve done it all, this is it.”

I think probably just not trying to be sexy and just being cool and confident in your movies. I think with a lot of my fans, I’ve gotten to a very, very cool place where there’s a direct line between the man they know off-screen and the man they see on-screen.”
