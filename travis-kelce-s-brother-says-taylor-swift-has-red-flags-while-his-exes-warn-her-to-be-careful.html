Swifties may be thrilled about Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce’s budding romance, but it appears his family has some concerns. Specifically, Travis’ brother Jason Kelce recently opened up about how safety has become an issue, given how much attention there is around Taylor and Travis’ new relationship.

Speaking to NBC Sports on October 22, the Philadelphia Eagles center said that while he’s excited to see his younger brother happy, there are some concerns in the back of his mind.

Travis Kelce’s Brother Thinks Dating Taylor Swift Is A Safety Issue

"It's certainly been weird, the level that it is now," Jason explained. "On one hand, I'm happy for my brother that he seems to be in a relationship that he's excited about, that he is genuine about. But there's another end of it where it's like, ‘Man, this is a lot.'"

Referring to a recent moment when paparazzi caught up to him while he was putting gas in his car, Jason admitted this "is another level of stardom that typically football players don't deal with."

"So, on one hand, really, really happy for my brother and where he's at in his current situation with Taylor," he went on. "But on the other hand, there's some alarms sometimes with how over-in-pursuit people can be."

But even though the media attention may be overwhelming for Travis as well as the rest of his family, the NFL star doesn’t seem dissuaded in his romance with Taylor.

"Overall, he can deal with some of this," Jason said of his brother. "As long as it's not becoming a threat to his safety and things like that."

Travis seems to be enjoying the newfound fame. He’s reportedly profiting from his connection to Taylor. Not only have his jersey sales increased, but he’s now got a larger following on social media, which can turn into higher-paid brand deals.

The athlete recently invested in a $6 million mansion that apparently has heightened security features to offer him more privacy as he continues his relationship with Taylor.

Travis Kelce’ Ex-Girlfriends Have Warned Taylor Swift About Him

But it’s not only Travis’ family that has concerns over their romance. Two of his ex-girlfriends have also seemingly issued a warning to Taylor, telling her to be careful considering Travis’ history.

Last month, his ex Maya Benberry gave an interview to Daily Mail where she accused Travis of cheating on her. She also implied he may be with Taylor primarily for the publicity she can offer him. “I’m sure by now she has mastered the ability to see who is really there for her - and who is just using her,” Maya told the outlet. “I can only hope that she comes out the teacher in this situation and not the student.”

Shortly after, Travis’ other ex Kayla Nicole spoke out amid a flurry of online hate she’s been receiving since he was connected to Taylor. Though she didn’t name-drop, Kayla seemed to imply their relationship didn’t end on the best of terms.

Despite the concern over their romance, Taylor and Travis still appear to be going strong.
