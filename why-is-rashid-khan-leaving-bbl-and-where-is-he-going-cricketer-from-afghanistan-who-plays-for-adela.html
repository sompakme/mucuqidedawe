Is Rashid Khan really leaving BBL amidst the ongoing rumors? Where is the cricketer actually going? Keep reading to know more.

Rashid Khan is an Afghan cricketer.

Known for his spin sensation, this young 23-year-old athlete became just the third man to claim a BBL six-wicket haul with remarkable figures.

Recently, Rashid Khan caught the public’s interest after the news about him leaving BBL surfaced on the web.

Why Is Rashid Khan Leaving BBL And Where Is He Going?

Rashid Khan is leaving BBL to play for his own country and he is going to Afghanistan.

According to Fox Sports News, he was set to leave the BBL for international duty after the clash between Mujeeb’s Brisbane Heat and Khan’s Strikers.

The athlete also talked about being unsure of how many games he will play but probably two more games and after that, he goes back for fulfilling the national duties.

Who Is Rashid Khan Wife?

Rashid Khan’s wife was once displayed as Anushka Sharma in the Google search bar as noted in Hindustan Times.

And. as most of us are aware, the actress Anushka Sharma is already married and is also with the athlete, But it’s not Rashid but Virat Kohli.

In 2020, Rashid Khan’s name was linked up with the actress and even Google showed her as his partner. Once, in an interview, he was asked about his favorite female actors. Rashid answered Anushka and Priety Zinta and that’s pretty much it.

As a matter of fact, Rashid khan is not married, thus, has no wife. But, we can not say the same thing with surety when it comes to his girlfriend.

Rashid Khan Net Worth Explored
